Frequently Asked Questions

How does your 72-hour closing process work?

Our expedited closing process begins with a same-day property evaluation, followed by a cash offer within 24 hours. Once you accept, we coordinate with our dedicated closing team to complete the transaction within 72 hours, subject to clean title.

What types of properties do you buy?

We purchase all types of residential properties in any condition - single-family homes, multi-family units, condos, and townhouses. Whether your property needs major repairs or has tenant issues, we're interested.

How do you determine your offer price?

Our offers are based on current market conditions, location, property condition, and necessary repairs. We provide transparent pricing breakdowns and ensure our offers are competitive for as-is, cash purchases.

What costs do I need to pay?

None. We cover all closing costs, legal fees, and commissions. The offer we make is the amount you receive at closing, with no hidden deductions or surprise fees.

Can you help with difficult situations?

Yes, we specialize in complex situations including foreclosure, probate, tax liens, divorce, and problem tenants. Our experienced team can guide you through any challenging real estate scenario.
